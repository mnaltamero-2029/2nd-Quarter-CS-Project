WHY IT'S AN ISSUE

    Unemployment is an issue as it threatens financial and economic stability, sometimes leading to social unrest.
    For individuals, self-sustenance and sustaining households becomes difficult without a stable source of income.
    Long periods of joblessness can erode skills, reduce future employability and place pressure on families and local services.
