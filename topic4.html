POSSIBLE SOLUTIONS

    Although unemployment is partly driven by automation and cost-cutting, lack of targeted training and local opportunities greatly worsens the problem.
    Solutions include expanding vocational and adult education, funding re-skilling programs, supporting entrepreneurship and small business growth, and creating regional employer–training partnerships that connect people with available work and on-the-job learning.
