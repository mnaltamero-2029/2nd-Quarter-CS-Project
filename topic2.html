CAUSES OF UNEMPLOYMENT

    Unemployment is caused by economic fluctuations which often prompt businesses to cut down on production, reducing demand for workers and/or services.
    It can also arise from structural changes such as automation and technological advances that reduce demand for certain skills, or from mismatches
    between available jobs and workers' skills or location.
